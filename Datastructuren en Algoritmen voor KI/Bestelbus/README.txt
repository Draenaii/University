Input:
	The first line asks for two positive integers. The first is the amount of packages you want to send (n), the second is the amount of 'deliveries' the packages have to be delivered in.
	It then asks for n more numbers, those are the weights of the packages. You can either enter it separated by spaces or by lines (or both ;P). The are being delivered in the given order.
Output:
	It gives the minimal capacity for the van in order to ship those packages in that amount of deliveries.
